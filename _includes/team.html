What is Map3?

The first decentralized map service network built by Hyperion and managed by a decentralized group of global community. It is open to participation to everyone and fault-tolerant to attacks. OpenStreetMap, an initiative that began 14 years ago has revolutionized how map data are created. Through years of efforts, there are currently millions of contributors and billions of edits made to its open map database. However, the world needs more than open map data, it also needs open map services.

We decided to take one step further - build an open network of map-service providing nodes and decentralize the way we create and consume map services. We can finally have privacy-preserving, free and consensus driven map data and services.
